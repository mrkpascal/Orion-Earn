Frequently Asked Questions

How do I withdraw my earnings?

You can withdraw your earnings by going to your Dashboard and clicking the "Withdraw via MoMo" button. You need to have a verified MTN Mobile Money account and at least the minimum withdrawal amount.

How much can I earn per video?

Earnings per video vary depending on the length and type of content. Typically, you can earn between 10-50 RWF per video watched.

Is there a limit to how many videos I can watch?

There's no strict limit, but to ensure fair usage, we may implement daily caps during peak times. We'll always notify you if any limits are applied.

About Us

Our journey from manual system to digital platform

Our Story

Orion Earn started as a small manual system where users would report their video views and shares, and we would manually process payments. This approach had limitations in scalability and user experience.

In 2025, we launched the fully digital version of Orion Earn, automating the earning and withdrawal processes. Our platform now integrates directly with MTN Mobile Money for instant payments and uses advanced tracking for video views and shares.

Our Mission

To empower people to earn online with simplicity. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to monetize their time and social networks in a fair and transparent way.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital earning platform in Africa, providing financial opportunities to millions of users across the continent.
